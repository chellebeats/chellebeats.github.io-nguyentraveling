My Time as a Chicagoan

Every year, my boyfriend and I always travel for our birthdays. This year I thought, "What better place to go than his hometown of Chicago?" I had never been and was excited to take in this Midwest metropolitan city.

Day 1

Our first day was so exciting! It was my first time to a MLB game and I got to experience it at Wrigley Field with the Chicago Cubs who just won the World Series the year before. It was kind of magical. The comradery, the beautiful weather, and the nolstagic stadium made this an experience I would never forget! Especially because we had the chance to keep a foul ball from one of the players. When I got the ball, I thought "What a great way to remember this trip!" Unfortunately, it hit the guy seated next to me in the neck before I grabbed it and I could not in good conscience keep it for ourself.

Day 2

We visited Millennium Park and the Art Institute of Chicago.

Both places are a must when visiting the city. I would recommend at least 3 hours for the museum.

Day 3

When traveling, we always like to add a day in for shopping. We visited the "hipster" neighborhood of Wicker Park to visit some local thrift stores. We also went to the Randolph St. Market Festival, which had lots of antique and vintage goodies.
